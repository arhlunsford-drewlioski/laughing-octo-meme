# Goals & Goblins - Full Goblin Roster (20)

## Asset Sizes
- Token (pitch): 256x256 PNG (displays at ~80px diameter)
- Card art: 480x660 PNG (displays at 160x220)

## Stat Range: 0-8 per stat. Total budget ~12-14 per goblin.
## Each goblin has a Base Passive (always active) and a Level 2 Passive (unlocked during run).

---

## GILDED CODEX (Possession focus - control and passing)

### 1. Xavi Hernandgob
- **Parody of:** Xavi Hernandez
- **Personality:** "Has completed more passes than words spoken. Both are in the thousands."
- **Stats:** ATK 2 / MID 7 / DEF 2 / GK 1
- **Base Passive:** Possession cards give +1 extra when in Midfield
- **Level 2:** Possession cards also give +1 to adjacent zone goblins
- **Unlock:** Starter goblin

### 2. Andrea Pirlog
- **Parody of:** Andrea Pirlo
- **Personality:** "Looks like he's barely moving. Somehow everywhere."
- **Stats:** ATK 1 / MID 6 / DEF 3 / GK 2
- **Base Passive:** Opponent Possession cards give -1 each
- **Level 2:** Also reduces opponent Defense cards by -1
- **Unlock:** Starter goblin

### 3. Luka Modgritch
- **Parody of:** Luka Modric
- **Personality:** "Tiny. Angry. Runs the entire midfield alone."
- **Stats:** ATK 3 / MID 6 / DEF 2 / GK 1
- **Base Passive:** Draws an extra card at round start if in Midfield
- **Level 2:** Extra card drawn is always a Possession card (if available)
- **Unlock:** Win 1 tournament

### 4. Tiki Takata
- **Parody of:** Spain's tiki-taka style (not one player)
- **Personality:** "Pass. Pass. Pass. Pass. Pass. Pass. Goal? No, pass."
- **Stats:** ATK 1 / MID 5 / DEF 4 / GK 2
- **Base Passive:** If 3+ Possession cards played in a round, gain +2 bonus Possession
- **Level 2:** Bonus increases to +4
- **Unlock:** Play 5 Possession cards in a single round

---

## SCREAMING TIDE (Tempo/Attack focus - pace and pressing)

### 5. Kylian M'Bappog
- **Parody of:** Kylian Mbappe
- **Personality:** "Blinks and he's past you. Blinks again and he's past the goalkeeper."
- **Stats:** ATK 7 / MID 3 / DEF 1 / GK 0
- **Base Passive:** Tempo cards gain +5% conversion when in Attack
- **Level 2:** +10% instead of +5%
- **Unlock:** Starter goblin

### 6. Erling Braut Hogland
- **Parody of:** Erling Haaland
- **Personality:** "Built like a siege tower. Kicks like one too."
- **Stats:** ATK 8 / MID 1 / DEF 2 / GK 1
- **Base Passive:** Tempo cards under 25% base gain +10% bonus
- **Level 2:** Threshold increases to under 35%
- **Unlock:** Starter goblin

### 7. Neymargh
- **Parody of:** Neymar
- **Personality:** "Has been rolling on the ground for 45 minutes. The ref is concerned."
- **Stats:** ATK 6 / MID 3 / DEF 1 / GK 0
- **Base Passive:** When a Tempo card misses, gain +1 energy next round
- **Level 2:** Also gain +1 Momentum on miss
- **Unlock:** Miss 3 Tempo cards in one round and still win

### 8. Thierry Hengrub
- **Parody of:** Thierry Henry
- **Personality:** "Glides past defenders like they're not there. They wish they weren't."
- **Stats:** ATK 6 / MID 4 / DEF 1 / GK 0
- **Base Passive:** Scoring a goal shifts Momentum +1 toward you
- **Level 2:** First goal each half shifts Momentum +2 instead
- **Unlock:** Score 3+ goals in a single match

## IRONCLAD BASTIONS (Defense focus - walls and counterattacks)

### 9. Virgob van Dijk
- **Parody of:** Virgil van Dijk
- **Personality:** "Nobody gets past. Nobody tries twice."
- **Stats:** ATK 1 / MID 2 / DEF 7 / GK 2
- **Base Passive:** Reduces opponent Tempo conversion by 3% when in Defense
- **Level 2:** Reduces by 5% instead
- **Unlock:** Starter goblin

### 10. Gianluigi Buffgon
- **Parody of:** Gianluigi Buffon
- **Personality:** "Has been goalkeeping since before the pitch was built."
- **Stats:** ATK 0 / MID 1 / DEF 2 / GK 8
- **Base Passive:** Once per match: negate a converted Tempo goal
- **Level 2:** Twice per match instead of once
- **Unlock:** Starter goblin

### 11. N'Goblo Kante
- **Parody of:** N'Golo Kante
- **Personality:** "Covers every blade of grass. Smiling the entire time."
- **Stats:** ATK 1 / MID 4 / DEF 5 / GK 1
- **Base Passive:** Defense cards give +1 extra when in Defense or Midfield
- **Level 2:** Also gives +1 Possession when Defense card is played
- **Unlock:** Block 10+ opponent Possession in a single match

### 12. Sergio Ramogrish
- **Parody of:** Sergio Ramos
- **Personality:** "Defends with passion. Fouls with more passion."
- **Stats:** ATK 3 / MID 1 / DEF 6 / GK 1
- **Base Passive:** After halftime, Defense cards cost 0 energy (once per round)
- **Level 2:** Also applies to the first Defense card before halftime
- **Unlock:** Win a match conceding 0 goals

## MIDNIGHT SKULK (Counter-attack focus - patience and punishing)

### 13. Gleon Messy
- **Parody of:** Lionel Messi
- **Personality:** "Walks around doing nothing for 80 minutes. Then destroys everything."
- **Stats:** ATK 5 / MID 5 / DEF 1 / GK 0
- **Base Passive:** Tempo cards gain +3% for each point of Momentum in your favor
- **Level 2:** Also +2% for each net Possession advantage
- **Unlock:** Starter goblin

### 14. Zinedine Zidunk
- **Parody of:** Zinedine Zidane
- **Personality:** "Elegant. Graceful. Will headbutt you without warning."
- **Stats:** ATK 4 / MID 5 / DEF 2 / GK 0
- **Base Passive:** Tactical cards have no DEF penalty
- **Level 2:** Tactical cards also give +1 Momentum
- **Unlock:** Win 2 tournaments

### 15. Ronaldgobinho
- **Parody of:** Ronaldinho
- **Personality:** "Does tricks instead of scoring. Scores anyway."
- **Stats:** ATK 5 / MID 4 / DEF 1 / GK 1
- **Base Passive:** Each round, one random card in hand costs 0 energy
- **Level 2:** Two random cards cost 0 instead
- **Unlock:** Win a match using only 1 energy or less in a round

### 16. Landon Donogob
- **Parody of:** Landon Donovan
- **Personality:** "Invisible for 80 minutes. Immortal for the last 10."
- **Stats:** ATK 4 / MID 3 / DEF 3 / GK 1
- **Base Passive:** Tempo cards gain +15% conversion in rounds 7-8
- **Level 2:** Also gain +10% in rounds 5-6
- **Unlock:** Score a winning goal in the final 2 rounds

## THUNDERING MAW (Power/Chaos focus - brute force and randomness)

### 17. Gristiano Bonaldo
- **Parody of:** Cristiano Ronaldo
- **Personality:** "SIUUUU! *flexes after every pass*"
- **Stats:** ATK 7 / MID 2 / DEF 1 / GK 1
- **Base Passive:** After scoring, all Tempo cards gain +5% for rest of match
- **Level 2:** +8% instead of +5%
- **Unlock:** Starter goblin

### 18. Wayne Groonbog
- **Parody of:** Wayne Rooney
- **Personality:** "Angry at the ball. Angry at the pitch. Angry at physics."
- **Stats:** ATK 5 / MID 3 / DEF 3 / GK 1
- **Base Passive:** When losing, all cards gain +1 to their primary stat
- **Level 2:** When losing by 2+, gain +2 instead
- **Unlock:** Come back from 2 goals down to win

### 19. Zlatan Gobrahimovic
- **Parody of:** Zlatan Ibrahimovic
- **Personality:** "Zlatan does not play for the team. The team plays for Zlatan."
- **Stats:** ATK 6 / MID 2 / DEF 2 / GK 2
- **Base Passive:** If he's the only goblin in his zone, +3 to that zone's stat
- **Level 2:** Bonus increases to +5
- **Unlock:** Win a match with a goblin solo in Attack zone

### 20. Peleg
- **Parody of:** Pele
- **Personality:** "The original. The greatest. Will not stop telling you about it."
- **Stats:** ATK 5 / MID 4 / DEF 3 / GK 2
- **Base Passive:** All other goblins on the team gain +1 to their zone stat
- **Level 2:** Gain +2 instead
- **Unlock:** Win 5 tournaments (final unlock, the GOAT reward)
